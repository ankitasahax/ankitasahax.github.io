Postures are the building blocks of Yoga. Our body can take innumerable postures. But all postures are not yoga. Postures which can elevate physical and mental capabilities are recognised as Yogic Postures. The beauty of Yoga is that you don't need to believe in anything, or you don't need to be Yogi or Yogini.


Yoga turns physical exercises into tools which help to find the ultimate expression of life. By mastering over your body, mind , emotion snd life energy, yogic tools that can turn itself into a carrier to achieve the blissful state of life which we are always longing for.
